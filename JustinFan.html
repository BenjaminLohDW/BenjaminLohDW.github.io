Biography

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. Discovered at a young age, he quickly rose to fame with his hit single "Baby" in 2010. Since then, Bieber has released multiple chart-topping albums and has become one of the most influential pop stars of his generation. Known for his unique voice, charismatic personality, and global fanbase, Justin continues to be a dominant force in the music industry.

Music

Justin Bieber's discography includes hits like "Sorry," "Love Yourself," "What Do You Mean?," and "Yummy." His albums include:

  • My World 2.0 (2010)
  • Believe (2012)
  • Purpose (2015)
  • Changes (2020)
  • Justice (2021)
